About Us

about us

With Scieline we aim to “maximize collaborative research through highly collaborative customer service”

The Scieline team is based in Israel and the United States and services the pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, chemical and food customers globally. The team has seen increasing interest in paperless solutions for R&D and continues to harness the ideas of its clients to improve Scieline and create a best in class platform for R&D.

Scieline was first developed to document work in the R&D laboratory for a global biopharmaceutical company based in EuropeWhat was unique at the time was the idea to mirror the manufacturing process at the product development state. By doing so we realized we could significant reduce product development timelines. Recognizing the power of Scieline, we decided to offer this solution more broadly and into different industry sectors. And since the initial introduction of Scieline in XXXX, a loyal customer base and dedicated support staff has grown around this software solution.
