about the project

Scieline recognizes that science is iterative and innovation is a function of reliable experimental replication at meaningful scale. It is often about connecting the dots and finding meaning in disparate data points. Scienline will help you connect the dots whether you are at the bench or managing a project far from the bench.
With Scieline you no longer worry about data being buried in a lab notebook or an excel spreadsheet. Scieline modules from sample management, electronic notebook and data analytics ensure data is always available for query, analysis and reporting

Importantly, Scieline for biologics is focused on the process, recognizing that process is of fundamental importance to ensuring product quality. For that reason, Scieline is configured to mirror your process

Whether selecting the most optimal cell line or understanding which variables yielded the best product profile, Scieline quickly becomes a powerful tool for the bench scientist and process engineer. It will also facilitate the handoff to regulatory teams preparing for product registration and registration maintenance or renewal
